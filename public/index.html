Privacy

Account Creation

Upon account creation, we encrypt and securely store user passwords, ensuring confidentiality and privacy. We have a strict policy against sharing or disclosing encrypted password information. Our commitment to robust encryption techniques ensures the security of user account credentials.

Messaging

All messages sent and received through our platform undergo encryption processes, ensuring that their contents remain confidential and secure. It's important to note that we do not store any messages in our database, adhering to a strict policy of non-retention. Additionally, messages are transmitted directly between users and do not pass through our servers, further enhancing privacy and minimizing the risk of interception. This approach underscores our commitment to protecting user data and fostering a secure environment for communication.

Local Storage Policy

Login

Our platform utilizes local storage to maintain user login sessions, ensuring seamless access to personalized features.Upon successful login, a session token is securely stored in the user's browser for a duration of one day, facilitating convenient access to their account during subsequent visits. This approach enhances user experience by eliminating the need for repeated logins within the session timeframe, while prioritizing security and privacy through the utilization of browser-local storage mechanisms.

User ID

Our platform leverages local storage to efficiently update and track user IDs for messaging functionality. Upon user engagement with the messaging feature, their unique identifier is stored locally within the browser environment, facilitating seamless communication and message delivery. By utilizing local storage, we optimize performance by reducing the need for frequent server requests while ensuring a responsive and interactive messaging experience for our users. This approach not only enhances the efficiency of our messaging system but also prioritizes user privacy by securely managing user data within the confines of their browser.
